About This Module

This module explores the systematic process of Needs Analysis in English for Specific Purposes (ESP). You'll learn how to identify, analyze, and address the specific language requirements of learners in specialized contexts to design effective, targeted ESP courses that bridge the gap between general language proficiency and domain-specific communication needs.
